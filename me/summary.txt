## About Pratik

Pratik is originally from Nepal and is currently focused on building agentic AI systems that solve real human problems.

He is not interested in simple demos or surface-level AI tools. His goal is to build production-grade AI applications that are reliable, useful, and capable of creating measurable impact in the real world.

Pratik thinks deeply about how AI can support people in practical areas such as healthcare, communication, workflow automation, and financial freedom.

## Current Project

### Laxora AI — Safe Healthcare Scheduling Agent

Pratik is currently building Laxora AI, an agentic AI system focused on healthcare scheduling and patient intake.

The first goal is to create a safe scheduling and guided intake experience that helps patients complete forms faster and with less confusion. The system is designed to reduce drop-off during intake by making the process smarter, more conversational, and easier to follow.

The long-term vision is to support healthcare providers by reducing front-office workload, improving patient communication, and creating a more efficient patient experience.

## Technical Approach

Pratik approaches AI development with a strong focus on reasoning, evaluation, and system reliability.

He believes an AI product should not only work in a happy-path demo. It should be tested against edge cases, failure modes, unclear user inputs, and real-world workflow complexity.

His approach includes:

- Reviewing model and agent architectures carefully
- Testing how the system reasons through different scenarios
- Evaluating safety, reliability, and accuracy
- Stress-testing edge cases before scaling
- Iterating based on real-world feedback
- Designing systems with privacy, security, and human trust in mind

## Philosophy

Pratik believes AI should be aligned with human outcomes.

For him, AI should not replace human dignity or human connection. It should help people live better lives by improving access, reducing friction, and supporting meaningful work.

His core belief is that AI should create value in areas that truly matter, including better healthcare, deeper human connection, and real financial freedom.
